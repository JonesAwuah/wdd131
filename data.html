Overview

Ghana is a West African country located along the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, stable democracy, and diverse landscapes ranging from lush forests to coastal savannas.

Quick Facts

  • Official Name: Republic of Ghana
  • Capital: Accra
  • Area: 238,535 sq km
  • Population: 34 million (2024 est.)
  • Official Language: English
  • Other Languages: Akan, Ewe, Ga, Dagbani, and others
  • Currency: Ghanaian Cedi (GHS)
  • Time Zone: GMT (no daylight saving)
  • Calling Code: +233
  • Internet TLD: .gh
  • Independence Day: March 6, 1957
  • Major Cities: Kumasi, Tamale, Takoradi, Cape Coast
