AJ Consult – Website Planning Document

Site Name

AJ Consult – A professional consultancy firm helping individuals and businesses in Ghana navigate regulatory compliance, documentation, and administrative processes.

Optional Domain: ajconsultghana.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide clear, accessible information about the services offered by AJ Consult, such as business registration, tax filing support, passport and visa advisory, and more. The site will include contact details, client testimonials, and an easy-to-use request form for consultations.

Scenarios

  • How can I register my new business with the Registrar General in Ghana?
  • What are the documents required for passport and visa applications?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color – Midnight Blue (#2c3e50): Used for headers, navigation, and footer background.
  • Accent Color – Gold (#f1c40f): Used for call-to-action buttons, links, and highlights.
